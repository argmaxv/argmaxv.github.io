Seongmin A Park

I am a project scientist in the team of Erie Boorman at the center for mind and brain in the University of California, Davis. I received my Ph.D. in Culture Technology with Jeaeseung Jeong at KAIST. I was a postdoctoral researcher in the team of Jean-Claude Dreher at CNRS.

My research focuses on learning and decision-making using computational modeling and neuroimaging techniques. I want to understand how the brain generalizes previous experiences and uses it for making a novel decision. I am interested in how our brain organizes everyday experiences, constructs structural knowledge and uses it to make a decision that we have never made before.
